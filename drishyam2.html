Jab We Met is a romantic comedy film that was released in 2007 in India. The film was directed and written by Imtiaz Ali and produced by Dhillin Mehta. The movie stars Shahid Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor in the lead roles, along with other supporting actors. The two meet on a train journey when Geet, who is upset about her boyfriend's absence, starts a conversation with Aditya. Despite his initial reluctance, Aditya starts to enjoy her company and begins to open up to her. When they arrive at the station, Geet discovers that she has lost her luggage and money, and Aditya reluctantly agrees to help her get to her destination. As they travel together, Aditya and Geet's personalities clash, but they also begin to develop a deep friendship. However, their journey takes unexpected turns as they face various challenges, including getting stranded in the middle of the night and getting into an accident. Aditya offers to take her back home, but she insists on going to Manali, where she has always wanted to go. They part ways, but Aditya's life is forever changed by the experience, and he becomes a happier and more confident person. Jab We Met is a heartwarming story of two people who find each other during a difficult time in their lives and help each other overcome their struggles. The movie is known for its beautiful cinematography, great music, and the excellent chemistry between the two lead actors. It is a feel-good movie that has become a classic in Indian cinema and is loved by audiences worldwide.
